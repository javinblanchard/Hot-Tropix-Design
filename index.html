Services

What we offer

Vehicle Lettering

Make your business stand out on the road with clean, professional vinyl lettering. From company names and logos to USDOT numbers and contact info — we’ll get your fleet noticed and compliant.

Vehicle Wraps and Decals

Turn your vehicle into a rolling billboard with custom wraps and decals. Whether it’s full coverage or simple branding, we’ll design and install graphics that boost visibility and leave a lasting impression.

Ceramic Coating

Protect your vehicle’s paint with our premium ceramic coating services. This advanced protective layer creates a deep, glossy shine while shielding against UV rays, road grime, and contaminants. It makes washing easier and keeps your finish looking brand new for years.

Paint Protection Film

Defend your paint from chips, scratches, and road debris with our self-healing Paint Protection Film. Virtually invisible, ultra-durable, and built to keep your finish flawless.

About

Who We Are

Hot Tropix Auto Spa is a family-owned business based in Westerville, OH, proudly serving the greater Columbus and beyond. We've been creating custom vinyl signs, vehicle lettering, and decals for over 25 years — and we bring that experience straight to you with fully mobile service.

  • Mobile service — we come to your home, shop, or job site
  • Over a decade of hands-on experience with vinyl graphics
  • Family-owned, friendly, and easy to work with

Whether you're a contractor who needs DOT numbers on your truck or a small business looking to upgrade your storefront windows, we’re here to help. Our goal is simple: make your business look sharp and stand out — without making things complicated.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

The easiest way to get started is to give us a call. Our team will walk you through the process, answer any initial questions, and schedule a consultation to discuss your project needs. From there, we’ll provide recommendations, pricing details, and a clear timeline so you know exactly what to expect.

Our workshop is based in Westerville, OH. However, you don’t need to travel to us— we specialize in mobile service. That means we come directly to your location for installation, making the process as convenient and seamless as possible.

Installation timelines typically range from 2 to 4 weeks, depending on the scope of the project, the complexity of the design, and material availability. We’ll provide you with a more precise estimate during your consultation, and we always strive to complete installations as efficiently as possible without compromising on quality.
